SwiftPaperless

Native document managament

SwiftPaperless is a native iOS app that interacts with the popular Paperless-NGX document management software. Paperless-NGX is self-hosted, and therefore fully privacy preserving: you control your data! With SwiftPaperless, it's easy to find, categorize and also add documents from your phone!
